Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United States?

Tacos were first introduced to the United States in 1905, when Mexican migrant workers brought them with them to work on railroads and other jobs. At first, tacos were considered a lower-class food, but they became mainstream as the children of migrant workers grew up and continued to eat them.
